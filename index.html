MY STORY

Hello world! My name is Edin Hamzić, and I'm a 30-year-old self-taught front-end developer currently residing in Graz, Austria. In late 2022, I embarked on a journey to learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript and React, and this website represents my first public project in the field. Prior to pursuing a career in web development, I obtained a degree from the University of Sarajevo, specifically from the Faculty of Criminal Justice, Criminology, and Security Studies. Over the course of my life, I have gained valuable experience through various job opportunities, which have provided me with a diverse range of skills in the business domain. In the section below, you will find a collection of small projects that I have worked on. These projects serve as demonstrations of my foundational knowledge and understanding of the programming languages I have learned. Although they may be modest in scope, they showcase my ability to apply key concepts and techniques effectively. Please take a moment to explore these projects and get a glimpse of my capabilities. If you have any questions or would like more information about a specific project, feel free to reach out.
